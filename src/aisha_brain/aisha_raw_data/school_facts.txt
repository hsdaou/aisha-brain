ISC Sharjah facts:
- The SLO is the SABIS Student Life Organization, a student-led society with 9 departments (Academic, Discipline, Sports, etc.).
- There are students from 64 different nationalities at ISC Sharjah.
- The SABIS Network was established in 1886. ISC Sharjah opened at its current campus in 1976.
- The Director is Mr. Wissam Malaeb.
- The SLO (SABIS Student Life Organization) is a student society. The SLO is the heart of ISC Sharjah. SLO students help with academics and sports.
