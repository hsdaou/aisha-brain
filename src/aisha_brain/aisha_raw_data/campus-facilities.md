# ISC-Sharjah Campus Facilities

## Sports Facilities
- Two semi-Olympic-sized swimming pools
- All-season running track and large sports field
- Two large indoor sports halls
- Dedicated KG gymnasium
- Outdoor basketball courts

## ITL Hall
The SABIS Integrated Testing and Learning (ITL) Hall has 580 computers. Students use it for exams and to revise and review material learned.

## Classrooms
All main classrooms for Grades 1 through 12 are equipped with Interactive Smart Boards. Academic facilities include a specialized art room and two music rooms.

## Science and Technology
- Three well-equipped science laboratories (biology, chemistry, physics)
- Two computer science rooms with state-of-the-art equipment

## Kindergarten Building
Kindergartners have their own independent building with colorful hallways, engaging classrooms, a large playground, and a special car track.

## School Theater
Professional-quality sound and lighting systems for music productions, presentations, and assemblies.

## Library
The library serves the school community with a suite of computers for online research. It has a wide range of fiction and non-fiction books in English, Arabic, and French.

## Cafeteria
A bright and modern cafeteria where students can enjoy food from home or purchase wholesome sandwiches and fresh fruit at break time or after school.

## Medical Clinics
Two medical doctors are on site at all times in modern, spacious clinics for student health needs.

## Campus Location
ISC-Sharjah is situated on a spacious campus on University City Road, Industrial Area 6, Sharjah, UAE.
