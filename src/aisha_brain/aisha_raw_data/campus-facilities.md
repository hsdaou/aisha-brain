#  Campus Facilities 
ISC-Sharjah is situated on a spacious campus, which includes **a state-of-the-art sports field and an all-season running track**. We have **two semi-Olympic-sized swimming pools** **with two large and well-equipped sports halls** , together with a dedicated **KG gymnasium** and outdoor **basketball courts**.
Our **school theater** has professional-quality sound and lighting systems that enhance music productions. We also have excellent amenities for presentations and assemblies.
**Kindergartners** enjoy their own, independent building with colorful hallways, engaging classrooms, a large playground, and a special car track. 
All of our **main classrooms** for students from Grades 1 through 12 are equipped with Interactive Smart Boards to assist student learning. Academic facilities include a specialized art room and two music rooms.
Our **Science Department** comprises three well-equipped science laboratories for biology, chemistry, and physics. We have two computer science rooms equipped with state-of-the-art computing equipment.
One distinctive feature of our school is our **SABIS ® Integrated Testing and Learning (ITL) Hall** with 580 computers, where students sit for exams and can revise and review material learned.
Our campus also includes a **library** that serves our school community, with a suite of computers where our older students may conduct online research or complete school assignments and research tasks. We have a wide range of fiction and non-fiction books in English, Arabic, and French.
We have a bright and modern **cafeteria** where our students can enjoy food they bring from home. Students can also purchase wholesome sandwiches and fresh fruit at break time or after school.
Our campus also has two **medical doctors** on site at all times, who have their own modern, spacious clinics where our students may go to be looked after in comfort in case of accident or illness.
We are very proud of our campus and the facilities that we offer our students. Our bright, well-equipped campus contributes to creating the very positive atmosphere we have in our school. **We do hope that you come to visit us!**
