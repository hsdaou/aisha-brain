##  Welcome to The International School of Choueifat - Sharjah 
_Where every child's potential is nurtured and their love of lifelong learning is inspired._
As a proud member of the global SABIS® Network – which includes The International Schools of Choueifat and SABIS® International Schools – we believe that every student can develop into a well-rounded individual, equipped with the skills to excel as future professionals and active contributors to a better world.
We believe in the pursuit of academic excellence. Our dedicated educators fulfill that philosophy by devoting time and care to each individual student, fostering a dynamic learning environment where students embrace curiosity, critical thinking, and a passion for knowledge.
## A World-Class Education, Right Here in the U.A.E.
The International School of Choueifat – Sharjah (ISC-Sharjah) opened its doors in 1976, becoming the first school to bring the world-class SABIS® Educational System to the U.A.E., and we are proud to be a private, non-selective, independent, English-medium, day school fully accredited by the government and international accreditation agencies. Our state-of-the-art campus and cutting-edge education technology provide the ideal setting for a world-class education that prepares students for the challenges and opportunities of the 21st century.
## Preparing Students for Global Success
Through our comprehensive, international curriculum, international curriculum – drawing from the best of American, British, and Emirati educational systems – students are empowered to excel in their academic pursuit and prepared for success in higher education. Our graduates are well-prepared to take a range of standarized exams, including the American (AP®, SAT), British (IGCSE, AS/A Level), and U.A.E. national exams. Beyond academics, our unique SABIS® Student Life Organization provides students with the opportunity to gain and refine life skills, empowering them to make a difference in their school and community.
## Experience the SABIS® Difference
We invite you to visit our beautiful campus, meet our exceptional faculty and staff, and discover the SABIS® difference for yourself. See firsthand how our approach to education fosters academic excellence, personal growth, and a love of learning.
We look forward to welcoming you to the ISC-Sharjah family.
