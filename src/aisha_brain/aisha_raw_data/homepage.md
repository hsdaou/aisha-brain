## About the School
The International School of Choueifat - Sharjah (ISC-Sharjah) opened its doors in 1976, becoming the first school to bring the SABIS Educational System to the UAE. It is a private, non-selective, independent, English-medium day school.

ISC-Sharjah has an enrollment of more than 4,000 students from over 64 different nationalities.

## Curriculum
ISC-Sharjah implements the SABIS Educational System, drawing from American, British, and Emirati educational systems. Students can sit for:
- SAT, TOEFL, AP exams (American pathway)
- IGCSE, O Level, AS and A Level exams (British pathway)

## Academic Levels
- Pre-Kindergarten and Kindergarten (KG 1 - KG 2)
- Lower School (Grades 1 - 5)
- Middle School (Grades 6 - 8)
- High School (Grades 9 - 12)
